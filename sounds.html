Sounds

First mix I made after moving out to California, based on a car ride with some friends around Half Moon Bay. Yeah, it was sunny that day.

Closing out the opening trilogy of Good Boys mixes, I wanted to showcase some diversity and make a textured, soulful mix inspired by my favorite producers and Dilla disciples. This is that.

A timeline of Southern rap, ranging from Three 6 Mafia affiliates to Erykah Badu and Frank Ocean. The intro features a Crunchy Black interview that was recorded in his living room a couple days after he was shot in the face.

Instant classic. When I first started mixing, I wanted to make short bursts of music with a cinematic arc, picking tracks most wouldn't think to lump together. "You shouldn't mix this with that." → "Watch me."
